Our Story & Purpose

Our Story & Purpose has been shaped by a deep commitment to transforming workplace safety in Africa.

Leading the HSE Transformation

Vanguard HSE Solutions (Pty) Ltd is a purpose-driven health and safety solutions company committed to transforming workplace safety across Africa through culturally aligned, technology-enabled, and people-centred approaches.

Founded by respected leaders Mabore Mabel Selepe and Dorothy Mabiletsa, we combine executive corporate experience, ESG leadership, and grassroots development expertise to deliver safety solutions that are not only compliant but meaningful.
